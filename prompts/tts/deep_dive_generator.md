* Broadcast-quality throughout — clear, purposeful, authoritative.
* In the context section at the start of the segment, the Anchor addresses

* In Q&A, questions are genuine. A slight forward lean on harder questions
  is audible in the pace.
* [pressing] and [with scepticism] signal a challenge, not just a question.

Expert:
* Warm but precise. Not performing warmth — actually engaged with the topic.
* Moves between analytical lenses without signalling the shift — the voice
  stays consistent as the angle changes.

  It is someone thinking carefully and choosing words deliberately.
* Confident without announcing it. When landing a sharp observation,
  slow very slightly — let it breathe before moving on.

  matters more than the surface suggests. [flatly] signals quiet certainty.

Pacing:
* Context block: measured and direct — the Anchor is informing, not chatting.
* Q&A: more fluid, natural variation as the conversation develops.

Accent: Neutral international English for both speakers.

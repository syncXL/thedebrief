### `<scientist>`
**What it does:** Evaluates the evidence quality behind a claim — what the data actually shows, what confidence level is warranted, and what is being oversimplified or ignored in public coverage.
**Best used for:** Health policy, climate events, pharmaceutical approvals, public health crises, environmental regulations, or any story making empirical claims that can be evaluated against research.
**Not for:** Stories where science is mentioned rhetorically but no empirical claim is actually being made.

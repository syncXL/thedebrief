### `<tech_analyst>`
**What it does:** Analyzes what a technology development unlocks, disrupts, or forecloses — focusing on impact to builders, users, and existing industries rather than the technology itself.
**Best used for:** Product launches, AI developments, infrastructure changes, platform policy shifts, startup activity, or regulation that directly targets a technology sector.
**Not for:** Stories where technology is background detail. A hospital buying new computers is not a tech story unless the technology itself is the subject.
